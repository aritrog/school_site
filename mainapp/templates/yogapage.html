The History of Yoga Day

The International Day of Yoga has been celebrated annually on 21 June since 2015, following its inception in the United Nations General Assembly in 2014.

Creation of Record

The first International Day of Yoga created a record for the largest yoga class, and another for the largest number of participating nationalities

Yoga 2020

The theme for 2020 is "Yoga at Home and Yoga with Family". While the world fights with coronavirus, Yoga will bring peace to the mind and strengthen us physically.

The first proposal

The idea of International Day of Yoga was first proposed by the current Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi, during his speech at the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA), on 27 September 2014.

The Adaption

On 11 December 2014, India's Permanent Representative Asoke Mukherji introduced the resolution in the UNGA. The draft text received broad support from 177 Member States who sponsored the text, which was adopted without a vote.

The first Yoga Day

The first International Day of Yoga was observed around the world on 21 June 2015. The Ministry of AYUSH made the necessary arrangements in India

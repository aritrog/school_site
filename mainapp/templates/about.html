Our Provisions

We are an English Medium Co-Educational School following the CBSE curriculum and providing education from classes Nursury to X. In 2020 we complete 12 years od educational service to the society.

Our Objectives

to promote and encourage and help in prosecution of studies and advancement of literary, cultural, political, religious, scientific and technical education in the socially and educationally lowest, down-trodden under privileged strata of our society.

Mission

M

To work and help for betterment and establishment of the people coming specially from scheduled caste, scheduled tribe, other backward class and minority communities.
To help the aged, sick helpless and indigent and suffered person, animals and other living creature as may be deemed appropriate.

Vision

V

To acquire, establish, starts, aid, run maintain or manage school, college, libraries, etc. for the benefit of the public
To arrange, demonstrate, share, impart and organise lectures, debates, discussions, seminars and documentary film through audio-visual materials & other aids for the benefit of the public and under-privileged community.

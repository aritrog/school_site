Brief biography of Dr. B.R.Ambedkar

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar (April 14, 1891 - December 6, 1956) was an Indian Jurist, Scholar, Bahujan Political Leader and a Buddhist revivalist, who is the chief architect of the Indian Contitutions. Born in a poor untouchable community, Ambedkar spent his life fighting against the system of Chaturvama and the Indian casts system.
